Jan 9, 2017

  • Class mechanics
  • Tambe: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  • Rice: Introduction to Social work
  • Rice/Tambe: An example Social Work + AI project
In both cases, we will introduce what is the history of the field, what are some major schools of thought, what are current trends. We will introduce basics, things taken for granted. For example, experiment design, IRB approval, search… A key topic of this class will be to get students to form teams. Teams must include students from social work and AI; while teaming arrangements could change over time, the initial team will be responsible for delivering potential topics for book chapters by Feb 6.
